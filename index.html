Waterproof Confidence

December 2016 & January 2017

SA Water VACSWIM is a government funded initiative for children aged 5 to 16 yrs to develop their skills and confidence in and around water.

The main SA Water Vacswim 2017 program will kick off on Tuesday 3rd January 2017 around South Australia and online enrolments will open on Thursday 1st September 2016. Until 2017 location dates are confirmed you can use 2016 as a general guide. Be sure to check the locations page for updates as they become confirmed.
